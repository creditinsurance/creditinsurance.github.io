What We Do

Group insurance industry professionals who focus on automating insurance application, underwriting and customer administration. Having years of experience in handling Group Insurance products such as Group Term Life Insurance, Mortgage Redemption Insurance and Credit Group Term Life Insurance. Most insurance companies exclude Group Insurance in their system for various reasons. System availability, maintenance and administration and cost.

Products under Group Insurance umbrella unlike individual insurance products has no fix mechanism, it adjust based on group portfolio that makes administration and maintenance difficult to handle as the transactions are more frequent than individual insurance. Systems perspective, there's no "one size fits all" application. Our system is flexible in adopting to different Group Product Structures, administration and maintenance, fast implementation and integration ready to other applications. Eliminating 80% of the manual processes for Group Insurance.

The system has a ready to use API which can be use to integrate to other sytems which eliminiates duplicate data entry. It has underwriting rules engine which automates the underwriting process and premium calculation, generates insurance certificates and medical referral forms, automates renewals, flexible reporting and a workflow integrated with clinics/hospitals.
